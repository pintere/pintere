IN A NUTSHELL WHO I AM

I would like to give an insight into who I am and how I identify myself.

I am a curious type of person who likes to explore new things (from travel to culinary novelties or even just a new way of walking home). My curiosity is leading my social relationships as well, I am the most happy when I'm around people who share their thougths, stories or feelings with me. I am working as a market and social researcher - using mostly social media data. I have been volunteering for most of my life, even when I was little, my mom took me with her to support women shelters. As a recreation I do yoga, I hike and (when the pandemic hit) I started to create concrete crafts and paint. Growing up in Hungary and socializing in a rather political enviroment, politics became a key focus of my interest.
